Method for screencapture

take with mobile

If you want to take screenshot with mobile, It's depending on your phone model, press the Power and Volume down buttons at the same time. If it dosen't work, press and hold the Power button for a few seconds. Then Screenshot. If neither of these work, go to your phone manufacturer's support site for help. At the bottom left, you will find a preview of your screenshot, then you can fix it that you want.

take with pc

If you use Mac, you can take screencapture by shift + command + 4 If you use Window, you can take screencapture by window + shift + s
